About "PM-Food For All" Yojna

This yojna is for the care of the below-poverty line people of India.In this act,Government of India is providing free food packets to all the below-poverty line people of India. Food Centers are installed in those areas where poverty ratio is much higher.

How It Is Working?

The government is taking the details of families and on that basis, they are calculating if they lie under the below-poverty line.Their adhaar card number and the phone number are registered.Now using these details the government can know if, there are migrants that lie below the poverty line as their phone numbers give their current location and adhaar-card gives their permanent address. In the case of any future disaster or any mis happening, the government can help those in need.
